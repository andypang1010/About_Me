Why did I decide to make a website?

I was going through some of the old files on my laptop a few weeks ago when I came across some photos that I took ages ago. They look old, without a doubt, but they still mean something to me, something very special. I don't know if anyone else would feel the same way, but at least that's the way I see it. Those are precious memories that I wouldn't want to relinquish.

It might be weird for some people to put all those dusty, old pictures on their website, but I disagree. In fact, those are actually the very moments that define who you are in the first place.
